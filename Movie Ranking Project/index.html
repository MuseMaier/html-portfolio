The Best Movies According to Marina

My top 3 movies of all-tame.

Spirited Away (2001)

My dear fellows, let me introduce the best anime I ever saw.
You will become a witness of a young girl whose life is turned upside down because of her parents.
Who will be her friend, who will be her foe, who will be her helper through the challenges?
Interested?
Then do not hesitate and watch this wonderful anime.

Howl's Moving Castle (2004)

What I really like about all of Hayao Miyazaki's work is that there is never a guess as to whether the hero is good or bad.
I suggest you watch and make up your own mind.

My Neghbor Totoro (1988)

Whether you like anime or not, this heartwarming story will win your heart!
The story of two sisters moving into the countryside who meet a forest spirit that is named Totoro.
Grab you blanket and pillow, make yourself comfortable, and get ready to dive into a world full of love, frienship and magic.
